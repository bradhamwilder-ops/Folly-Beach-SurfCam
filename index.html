Revenue Roadmap

The game plan to make this site worth more

The next step is not adding more ads. It is making the site more useful, then selling fewer placements at higher quality.

01

Finish the flagship experience

Replace the placeholder livestream, add real sponsor logos, and keep the homepage fast, sharp, and mobile-friendly.

02

Add daily-use utility

Tides, wind, sunrise, and a quick local note create repeat checking behavior, which is what sponsors actually pay for.

03

Sell exclusive category sponsorships

One sponsor per audience moment feels premium. It also makes the sales conversation simpler and more valuable.

04

Expand only after the first 3-5 sponsors

Once the homepage and category pages are full, add a small community partner strip instead of filling the site with low-value inventory.
