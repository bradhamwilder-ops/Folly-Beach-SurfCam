Washout Note

Surf context people care about

The Washout is widely known as one of Folly Beach’s top surf spots and is commonly noted as working best around mid- to high tide with north or northwest winds.

Business Model

Free for the public, ad-supported for the business

The goal is not to charge consumers. The goal is to build a useful daily destination, keep it free, and monetize it through sponsor placements for Folly Beach businesses that benefit from coastal traffic.

For Visitors

Live cam, tides, fishing, and local info

Give people a reason to return often by making the site genuinely helpful and quick to use.

For Advertisers

Paid placement next to real attention

Restaurants, surf shops, rentals, guides, and real estate brands can sponsor the traffic instead of relying only on social posts.

For Growth

More content means more sponsor inventory

Fishing, history, lodging, and local business pages create more surfaces to sell without putting up a paywall.
