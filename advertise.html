Sponsor System

How to make the ads look expensive

Sales Process

How to pitch this without overcomplicating it

The easiest sales story is simple: this is a daily-use Folly Beach homepage, and your business can own one of the most relevant local audience moments on it.

01

Show the homepage first

Lead with the camera, the design quality, and the presenting sponsor placement so the business immediately understands the vision.

02

Match the business to the right category

Restaurants fit broad beach traffic. Surf shops fit conditions. Guides fit fishing. Rentals fit travel planning.

03

Sell the slot as exclusive

The message is not “buy an ad.” It is “own this audience moment before someone else does.”

04

Ask for a 60-day test

Lower friction with a short launch term, then renew once the site has more utility, traffic, and proof points.
