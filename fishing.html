Regulations Snapshot

Bag limits and minimum sizes

Snapshot based on South Carolina saltwater regulations as available on March 23, 2026. Always verify before keeping fish.

Red Drum

15" to 23" TL

2 per person / 6 per boat State waters. Possession prohibited in federal waters.

Spotted Seatrout

14" TL minimum

10 per person / day Gig harvest closed Dec. 1 through Feb. 28.

Flounder

16" TL minimum

5 per person / 10 per boat Applies to hook and line or gig harvest.

Sheepshead

14" TL minimum

10 per person / 30 per boat Strong fit for dock, jetty, and structure content.

Black Drum

14" to 27" TL

5 per person / day Useful for marsh, dock, and bottom-fishing coverage.

Whiting / Spot / Croaker

No size listed

50 aggregate / person Good surf-fishing education content for visiting families.

Tripletail

18" TL minimum

3 per person / 9 per boat Seasonal nearshore interest species.

Spanish Mackerel

12" FL minimum

15 per person / day Popular warm-season nearshore and pier target.
Measurement note TL means total length. FL means fork length. Measure from closed mouth to tail according to South Carolina’s saltwater guidance.
Official source Verify limits on the South Carolina finfish limits page and through SCDNR.
